Frequently Asked Questions

What are your repair service hours?

Our repair services are available during our standard business hours: Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM, Saturday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Sunday from 11:00 AM to 5:00 PM. For urgent repairs outside these hours, please call us to make special arrangements.

How long does a typical repair take?

Most common repairs like screen replacements or battery changes are completed within 2-3 hours while you wait. More complex repairs like motherboard issues may take 24-48 hours. We'll always provide you with an estimated completion time when you bring in your device.

Do I need an appointment for repairs?

While appointments are not required, we strongly recommend scheduling one to minimize your wait time, especially during peak hours. You can book an appointment through our website or by calling our store directly.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile money payments. All major cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve) are accepted. We also offer payment plans for more expensive repairs - ask our staff for details.

Do you offer warranty on repairs?

Yes! We stand behind all our repairs with a lifetime warranty on labor and a 90-day warranty on parts. If you experience any issues with your repair, simply bring it back and we'll fix it at no additional cost.
