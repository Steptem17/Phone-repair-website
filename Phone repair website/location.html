Getting Here

By Car

We have dedicated parking spaces available for customers. From the Lagos-Abeokuta Expressway, turn onto Adura Road and look for our signage.

By Bus

Take the BRT bus to Adura Bus Stop. We're just a 3-minute walk from the bus stop, opposite the Alagbado Market.

By Train

The closest train station is Iju Station. From there, take a taxi or ride-hailing service to our location (about 10 minutes).
