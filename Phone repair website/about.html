Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming Lagos' most trusted mobile repair specialists

Redefining Mobile Repair Standards Since 2015

Founded in 2015 by tech enthusiast Chinedu Okonkwo, Freecom Technology began as a small repair kiosk in Adura Alagbado with a simple mission: to provide honest, high-quality repairs at fair prices.

What started as a one-person operation has grown into Lagos' premier mobile solutions provider with multiple locations across the city. Our growth has been fueled by an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction, technical excellence, and continuous innovation in repair methodologies.
