Comprehensive Phone Repair & Upgrade Services

At Freecom Technology, we provide top-tier mobile repair services to keep your device performing at its best. Our certified technicians specialize in fast, reliable, and high-quality repairs for all major brands.

Screen Replacement

Cracked, shattered, or unresponsive screen? A damaged screen can affect touch responsiveness, display quality, and overall usability. We offer high-quality replacements using durable, original-grade screens that restore full functionality and clarity. Our fast service ensures your device looks and feels brand new again, usually within an hour.

Battery Replacement

Is your phone constantly running out of battery, overheating, or shutting down unexpectedly? Over time, batteries degrade, causing reduced efficiency and shorter usage time. We replace old batteries with brand-new, high-capacity ones that provide long-lasting power, improved performance, and a more stable charging experience. Say goodbye to low battery anxiety!

Charging Port Repair

Having trouble charging your device, experiencing slow charging, or noticing your cable only works at a certain angle? A faulty charging port can be caused by dust buildup, damaged pins, or internal wiring issues. We thoroughly inspect, clean, and repair or replace your charging port to restore fast, efficient charging without interruptions.

Software Troubleshooting

Is your device stuck on a boot loop, freezing, or running slower than usual? We diagnose and fix software-related issues such as operating system crashes, app malfunctions, and security threats. Whether your phone needs a fresh software installation, firmware update, or virus removal, our experts ensure a smooth, optimized, and fully functional device.
